Horticulture at Chitkara University

Horticulture at Chitkara University plays a vital role in maintaining the campus's green and serene environment. The university emphasizes sustainable landscaping and biodiversity, fostering a healthy and vibrant ecosystem for all.

Plant Nursery

Our plant nursery is where the journey of many plants begins. The nursery houses young plants and saplings that are nurtured under expert care before being planted around the campus. From native species to unique flora, the nursery ensures a continuous supply of greenery to keep our campus lush and vibrant.

Greenery Maintenance

The Horticulture Department takes great pride in the ongoing maintenance of the university's green spaces. Our team regularly prunes, waters, and monitors plant health, ensuring a visually appealing and sustainable environment. By using environmentally friendly practices, we aim to make our campus a model of green living.

Herbal Garden

Our Herbal Garden is a peaceful haven on campus, featuring a variety of medicinal and aromatic plants. It serves as a hub for learning, research, and connecting with nature, promoting sustainability and well-being.

Sculpture Garden

The Sculpture Garden showcases stunning artworks surrounded by lush greenery. It offers a perfect blend of art and nature, inspiring creativity and providing a serene space for reflection and relaxation.

Rose Garden

Our Rose Garden is a vibrant attraction on campus, featuring a variety of colorful roses in full bloom. It adds natural beauty and tranquility, creating a perfect spot for relaxation, inspiration, and photography amidst nature's charm, while also serving as a serene venue for campus events and gatherings.
