Infrastructure at Chitkara University

Computer Science Department

Equipped with advanced labs, high-performance clusters, and expert faculty, our department offers hands-on training in AI, data science, and cybersecurity, preparing students for dynamic tech careers. Students can work on real-world projects and research, gaining valuable skills in high-demand fields.

Our facilities include cutting-edge technology labs and industry-grade resources for AI, data science, and cybersecurity. The department is housed in a state-of-the-art building, divided into two equal sections: the Ramanujan Block, dedicated to innovation and research, and the C.V. Raman Block, focused on practical, hands-on learning. Together, they provide an ideal environment for academic excellence and technical development.

Computer Application Department

Our Computer Application Department provides cutting-edge programs in software development, mobile app development, and database management. With expert faculty and industry-aligned curriculum, we equip students with the skills needed to succeed in the fast-evolving tech industry.

The department's facilities include advanced computer labs, access to industry-grade software, and hands-on project opportunities. The building housing our department is split into two sections: the Ramanujan Block, designed to foster research and innovation, and the C.V. Raman Block, dedicated to practical learning and development, creating a comprehensive learning environment.

B.Sc Nursing Department

The B.Sc Nursing Department is dedicated to providing a high-quality education in nursing with a focus on patient care, clinical skills, and healthcare management. Our curriculum is designed to ensure that students are well-prepared for the challenges of the healthcare industry.

The department is equipped with state-of-the-art nursing labs, simulation rooms, and a dedicated clinical training facility. The building that houses our department is divided into two sections: one focusing on academic learning and the other on hands-on clinical experience, creating a holistic environment for nursing students to grow and excel in their profession.

Pharmacy (B. Pharm) Department

The B. Pharm Department offers an extensive curriculum in pharmaceutical sciences, combining theoretical knowledge with practical experience in drug development, pharmacology, and healthcare management. Our focus is on preparing students to become skilled professionals in the ever-evolving field of pharmacy.

The department is housed in a state-of-the-art building named after Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, a symbol of innovation and dedication to education. The facility is designed with advanced laboratories, research spaces, and modern classrooms to provide an ideal learning environment for aspiring pharmacists. The building is divided into distinct sections for academic and practical training, ensuring students receive comprehensive exposure to both theoretical and hands-on aspects of pharmacy practice.

Library

The library building of our college is named after the legendary poet, Rabindranath Tagore, symbolizing the pursuit of knowledge, culture, and creativity. The space is designed to inspire students and provide them with an enriching environment for learning.

With a vast collection of books, academic journals, and digital resources, the library serves as a central hub for research, self-study, and intellectual growth. It fosters a peaceful and productive atmosphere for students to explore, learn, and innovate.

Laborator

Our college is equipped with advanced facilities to support diverse academic disciplines. Technical students benefit from state-of-the-art Computer Labs, Makers' Lab for innovation and prototyping, DSMA Lab for data analytics, and MCP Lab for microprocessor programming and embedded systems.

Healthcare students have access to specialized labs like the Pharmacy Lab for practical pharmaceutical training and the Nursing Lab, which simulates real-world clinical environments. These resources ensure students gain hands-on experience, bridging the gap between theory and practice.

Hostel Facilities

Our hostels provide a comfortable and safe living environment for students. Equipped with modern amenities, the hostels ensure that students have all they need for a productive and enjoyable stay during their time at the university.

The hostel facilities are divided into separate blocks for male and female students, each featuring spacious rooms, study areas, recreational zones, and round-the-clock security. Located close to academic buildings, the hostels offer easy access to campus facilities while ensuring a peaceful environment for rest and relaxation.

About Us

Chitkara University is committed to excellence in education, innovation, and ethical practices. Located in the scenic state of Himachal Pradesh, we aim to nurture the next generation of leaders.
