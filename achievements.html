Our Achievements

Ranked One of the Top Universities in India

Chitkara University has once again demonstrated its commitment to academic excellence, as reflected in the National Institutional Ranking Framework NIRF 2024 Rankings. The university's impressive performance across multiple categories highlights its continuous dedication to innovation, quality education, and industry relevance, reinforcing its status as a leading institution in India.

NAAC 'A+' Accreditation

The university has been accredited with an 'A+' grade by NAAC, reflecting its commitment to maintaining high standards in teaching, research, and infrastructure.

Go Beyond and Achieve Excellence

With 1500+ recruiters and countless success stories, our graduates are shaping the future in leading companies worldwide. Explore the opportunities that await you, backed by a network of industry giants

International Research Collaboration

We have partnered with prestigious universities worldwide, resulting in over 200 joint research publications and projects in the past five years.

Recipient of the Prestigious Gold Award at the 13th OCI-DL Shah Quality Award

Recognized by the Quality Council of India for fostering a culture of excellence, innovation, and continuous improvement. This accolade highlights our dedication to creating a world-class ecosystem for quality education, research, and development, empowering students to become future leaders in their fields.

A Close Bond with City Beautiful, Chandigarh

Nestled near the heart of Chandigarh, our campus thrives in an environment that blends academic excellence with the vibrancy of one of India's most beautiful and well-planned cities. A perfect setting for learning, growth, and inspiration.

CEED: Chitkara University Himachal Pradesh Startup Program

Fostering innovation and entrepreneurship, CEED empowers aspiring innovators to turn ideas into reality. With robust mentorship, resources, and support, the program is a launchpad for startups to thrive and make an impact in the ever-evolving business world.

Leaders in Innovation and Sustainability

Ranked among the top universities in India for filing maximum patents, Chitkara University continues to drive innovation. Recognized for our eco-friendly practices, we are proud recipients of the prestigious Green Campus Award, showcasing our commitment to a sustainable future.

About Us

Chitkara University is committed to excellence in education, innovation, and ethical practices. Located in the scenic state of Himachal Pradesh, we aim to nurture the next generation of leaders.
